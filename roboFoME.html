RoboFoME random card generator

Now with 20% less gibbering madness!

RoboFoME is a random card generator inspired by RoboRosewater. It uses frequency analysis to randomly generate statistically accurate (but semantically meaningless) Magic cards from FanOfMostEverything's Friendship is Card Games set. Any playable cards generated are purely accidental.
